MTHS HACKATHON



4/24-4/25


HackMTHS is Monroe Township High School’s first (virtual) hackathon. HackMTHS caters specifically to introductory coders with multiple beginner workshops such as Intro to Cloud Computing, Intro to Java, and more. Any middle school or high school student can attend. Throughout HackMTHS, hackers will be pushed to think outside of the box in order to solve problems in the world around them with code.

FAQ


Q: What is a hackathon?

A: A hackathon is a programming competition ranging from 12 hours to several days in length. Projects are built during this time period and judged. It’s a great chance for those interested in technology to develop their coding skills and win prizes along the way.


Q: How does a virtual hackathon work

A: As a result of COVID-19, organizations have been holding online hackathons to substitute the traditional version. Participants will be able to submit their projects through Devpost and provide a presentation either pre-recorded or live via a video conferencing platform. A panel of judges will listen to and review both the project submissions and presentations.


Q: Who can attend HackMTHS?

A: Any middle school or high school student can attend HackMTHS. You do not need any experience in coding to attend.


Q: How much does it cost to attend?

A: HackMTHS is free to attend!


Q: Will there be prizes?
A: Yes, right now our prize pool has not been announced. However, the first place prize will be around $100, the second place prize will be around $75, and the third place prize will be around $50.


Q: How will communications work?

A: We’ll be creating a Discord server where you can ask questions, read important updates, and chat with other hackers! Additionally, we’ll be posting information on how to join workshops and the introduction/closing ceremonies in the server.


Q: How will teams be formed?

A: You can work by yourself or work in a team with up to 4 people. If you have trouble forming a team, you can make a note in your pre-registration form. We’ll add you to a special channel on the Discord so that you can meet possible teammates.


Q:What if I need more time for handing in a project?

A: Reviewed on a case by case basis. Only applicable if there is a legitimate reason for an extension.
